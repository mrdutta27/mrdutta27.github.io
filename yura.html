The Need For Research Communities

Research is not meant to be a solitary endeavor. Every day, researchers around the world are trying to discover new truths and create revolutionary ideas in all disciplines from the physical sciences and engineering, to the humanities, social sciences, and beyond. The work of a single thinker is seldom as inspired as the work of multiple ideas and inspirations.

Connections that are formed through the research communities that we have created can transform the influence of undergraduate work by encouraging interdisciplinary discourse, guiding newcomers with mentorships and resources to jumpstart their research careers, and sparking new ideas that can change the world.

What Our Organization Provides

YURA provides an interdisciplinary community for both new and experienced undergraduate researchers to share dialogues, resources, and ideas, as well as connect with faculty members for advice and mentorship. To this end, we provide a platform for this communication, as well as multiple programs to connect undergraduates both on campus and nationwide.

Goals:
  • To foster interdisciplinary conversations between undergraduate researchers at Yale
  • To provide advice relating to various aspects of the research process in the form of workshops, written guides, and/or videos
  • To develop a comprehensive listing of Yale undergraduate research opportunities for the summer and term-time
  • To showcase opportunities for undergraduates to present their research
  • To create an undergraduate network between Yale and other research institutions

History

The Yale Undergraduate Research Association (YURA) was founded in January 2015 by myself and 4 other co-founders. Since our launch, we have grown to over 1500 members.

Our first event, the 2015 Yale Undergraduate Research Showcase, featured 60 interdisciplinary presenters and 400 audience members. Since then, we have hosted workshops and mixers, created a comprehensive database of research opportunities at Yale, and organized the first national undergraduate research conference to be hosted at Yale.
