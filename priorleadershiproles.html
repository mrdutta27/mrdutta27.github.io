Prior Leadership Roles

High School and College Leadership Opportunities

Yale Undergraduate Science Olympiad

Head Tournament Director | August 2014 - May 2015

Science Olympiad is a national non-profit organization dedicated to improving the quality of K-12 science education through participating in Science Olympiad tournaments. At Yale University, we host an annual invitational tournament with 50 teams from around the US. As Head Tournament Director, my job was to ensure the quality and integrity of all aspects of the exams given at the tournament to provide the maximum level of enrichment and practice for high school student competitors.

Chattahoochee HS Science Olympiad

President | August 2013 - May 2014

Chosen as President in Senior Year of High School, with over 6 years of experience competing in Science Olympiad tournaments at the regional, state, and national level. Under leadership, Chattahoochee HS hosted its inaugural Division B Invitational Tournament, with over 20 teams and 400 students participating. In addition, I organized a trip to the inaugural Yale Undergraduate Science Olympiad invitational tournament, where our team placed 4th in a field of 50 teams. At the state tournament, we placed 3rd with our strongest performance to date.

Chattahoochee HS TSA and VEX Robotics

Founder and President | January 2013 - May 2014

In Junior Year, founded the Chattahoochee HS Chapter of the Technology Student Association (TSA), a national organization of students engaged in science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) who participate in academic challenges at conferences hosted at the state and national level. As part of the TSA initiative, founded and led the VEX Robotics competitive team. Both our TSA chapter and our VEX Robotics team won acclaim under my leadership, with such accolades as 3rd place in state for VEX Robotics, 1st place in state and 12th place in nation for Technology Bowl, 1st place in state for Technical Problem Solving, and many others.

Chattahoochee HS Chess Team

Founder and President | August 2010 - May 2014

Founded the Chattahoochee HS Chess Team in Freshman Year, with more than 6 years of tournament chess experience, to bring both competitive and recreational chess to Chattahoochee HS. Organized award-winning competitive chess teams to play in tournaments around the year, winning multiple accolades at the regional and state level. Best performance: 3rd Place at the High School State Championship in 2011 and 2013.
